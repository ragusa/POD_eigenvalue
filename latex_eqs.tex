Eigenvalue problem
\[
A x = \lambda Bx
\]
where
\[
A = \sum_m 
\begin{bmatrix}
D_1^m K^m & 0\\
0 & D_2^m K^m \\
\end{bmatrix}
+
\begin{bmatrix}
\sigma_{r,1}^m M^m & 0\\
-\sigma_{s,1\to2}^m M^m & \sigma_{r,2}^m M^m \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]
and
\[
B = \sum_m 
\begin{bmatrix}
\nu\sigma_{f,1}^m M^m & \nu\sigma_{f,2}^m M^m \\
0 & 
\end{bmatrix}
\]
with $K^m$ and $M^m$ the stiffness and mass matrices in each material zone $m$.
